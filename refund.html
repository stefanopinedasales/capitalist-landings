Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-23

We want customers to be satisfied with Capitalist Engine. This policy describes how refunds are handled for purchases made through our checkout.

How to request a refund

Email support@capitalistengine.ai and include:

  • The email used at checkout
  • The product or service purchased
  • Reason for the request

Eligibility

Refund eligibility depends on the product or service purchased and the circumstances of the request. If a refund is available, we will process it back to the original payment method.

Timing

Approved refunds are typically submitted within a few business days. Your bank or card issuer may take additional time to post the credit.

Chargebacks

If you have a billing issue, please contact us first so we can help resolve it quickly.
